About us

Aek Ads is an emerging force in the AdTech space, transforming how audiences engage with media in the physical world. We combine cutting-edge technology with smart infrastructure to deliver impactful, real-time digital communication in residential societies, public spaces, and urban environments.

Our core strength lies in merging interactive experiences, programmatic advertising and data-driven insights across a growing network of digitally connected screens and systems. With a mission to build smarter cities and more engaged communities, Aek Ads offers advertisers, governments, and developers an intelligent platform to connect with people where it matters most — right where they live, move, and interact.

Mission

To empower brands, communities, and governments through intelligent digital media infrastructure that blends technology, creativity, and engagement.

We aim to create a seamless bridge between information and promotion — empowering city governance, enriching society communication, and elevating brand storytelling. Through innovation, service, and scale, we aspire to lead the next wave of digital transformation in India’s urban and residential landscapes.

Vision

To be India’s most trusted and innovative AdTech company, redefining the way information and advertising coexist in smart urban spaces.

We envision a world where public and private spaces are not only informative but also interactive — powered by technology that delivers value to citizens while enabling brands to communicate meaningfully and responsibly.

Infrastructure

Aek Ads’ modern, tech-enabled workspace designed to foster innovation. Located in a well-connected urban area at Gandhinagar Gujarat, Aek Ads houses dedicated departments for operations, creative design, R&D, and customer support, ensuring seamless execution of campaigns and technical excellence.

Aek Ads is backed by a robust infrastructure designed to support high-performance digital operations and large-scale deployments:

Display Network:

A growing fleet of digital displays installed across residential societies, corporate parks, and public Spaces PAN India.

Command Center & Ops Team:

A dedicated operations center monitors all devices, ensuring live status checks, instant content updates, and proactive technical support.

Field Maintenance Team:

A nationwide team of trained engineers and technicians ensures quick response times and routine servicing across all installation sites.

R&D and Production Facility:

In-house assembly and research unit for customizing screen hardware, testing new modules, and integrating future-ready technologies.
