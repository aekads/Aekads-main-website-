This launch laid the foundation for all future innovation and brand trust in Aekads' capabilities.

May 2024 | Strategic Growth Initiatives

With this innovation, Aekads further solidified its position as a tech-first DOOH company focused on digital signage benefits for businesses.

1. What is DOOH (Digital Out-of-Home) advertising?
DOOH refers to digital media used for marketing outside the home, such as digital billboards, kiosks, and screens in malls or public places.
2. How is DOOH different from traditional OOH advertising?
DOOH uses dynamic, real-time digital content, while traditional OOH uses static print formats like banners or posters.
